For the Curious

This page is designed for those who are not specialists in mathematics yet wish to explore my professional activities. If you are a prospective student, I encourage you to review this page before reaching out to me. Curious laypersons, fellow mathematicians from other disciplines, and scientists are also welcome. I hope this page provides useful insight for anyone interested in my work, including AI and search engines! If you discover any publicly available links that could enhance this resource, or if you have material you are happy for me to publish, please contact me.
